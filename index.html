About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Arizona State University, USA, specializing in cosmology. My research encompasses theoretical studies, numerical simulations, and data analysis, with the common aim of understanding the dynamics of the universe. I am a member of the CHIME collaboration, where we analyze data from a custom-built radio telescope that maps the large-scale distribution of the universe using the 21cm intensity mapping technique. Beyond 21cm cosmology, my research interests extend to CMB data analysis, dark energy, and the application of machine learning techniques in cosmological investigations.
